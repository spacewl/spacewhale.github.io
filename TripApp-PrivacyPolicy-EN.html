Privacy Policy 4Trip

This privacy policy explains how the 4Trip mobile app (hereinafter referred to as "we," "our," or "4Trip") collects, uses, and processes your personal information when you use our app. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring that your data is processed in accordance with the law.

  1. What information we collect
    When using 4Trip, we may collect the following data:
  • Personal information you provide during registration or use of the app (e.g., your name, email address, phone number, etc.).
  • Information about your use of the app, such as search history, reviews, saved locations, etc.

  1. How we use your information
    We use the collected information for the following purposes:
  • To provide, improve, and develop our services.
  • To analyze user activity to understand how our users use the app and to enhance its functionality.
  • To communicate with you, respond to your inquiries, send marketing materials, and other messages.

  1. Sharing your information
    We may share your personal information with our partners, suppliers, or other third parties under the following circumstances:
  • To provide services or support related to the app that you expect from us.
  • In case of a merger, sale, or transfer of assets of the company that owns 4Trip.
  • If required by law or law enforcement agencies.

We may also share aggregated or anonymized information that cannot be used to identify you personally for our marketing, analytical, or research needs.

  1. Storage and protection of information
    We store your personal information electronically on our secured servers. We take all necessary measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, loss, or misuse.

  1. Children's privacy protection
    The 4Trip app is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children who have not reached the age of 13.

  1. Changes to the privacy policy
    We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page. We recommend periodically reviewing this privacy policy to stay informed about our practices regarding the collection, use, and sharing of your information.

  1. Contact information
    If you have any inquiries or concerns about our privacy policy or the use of your personal information, please contact us at: developer@spacewl.com

  1. Account deletion You can delete your 4Trip account at any time by contacting us using the contact information provided in section 7. Upon deletion of your account, your personal information will be removed from our servers.
